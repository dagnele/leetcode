# Mission: LeetCode Problem Solving

## Why
Build stronger algorithmic problem-solving skill over the next month, not just memorize solutions. The target is to become more reliable at recognizing patterns, deriving approaches, and implementing correct solutions under a short daily practice constraint.

## Success looks like
- Solve most familiar-pattern Medium problems within 30-45 minutes.
- Explain the pattern, invariant, complexity, and edge cases of each solved problem.
- Review failed problems without copying editorials, then retry them from memory later.
- Maintain a repeatable 30-minute daily practice loop for one month.

## Constraints
- Current level: can solve some Medium problems but needs stronger pattern fluency and speed.
- Daily time: 30 minutes.
- Primary motivation: general coding skill, not immediate interview cramming.

## Out of scope
- Hard-problem grinding before Medium fundamentals are consistent.
- Competitive-programming-only topics that rarely improve LeetCode Medium performance in one month.
- Long passive video watching without solving or recall.
