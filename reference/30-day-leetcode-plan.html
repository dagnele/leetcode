30-Day LeetCode Plan

Goal: become more reliable at Medium problems with only 30 minutes per day. The win is pattern recognition plus clean review, not raw problem count.

5 min
Recall yesterday's pattern, invariant, and one edge case before opening today's problem.
18 min
Attempt one focused problem. If stuck, write what state you track and what changes per step.
7 min
Review: summarize pattern, complexity, mistake, and retry date. Do not just read a solution.

Problem Review Template

  • Pattern: What family is this problem really testing?
  • State: What variables/data structures carry the useful information?
  • Invariant: What remains true after each iteration or recursive call?
  • Failure: What specifically blocked me?
  • Retry: Try again in 2 days, then 7 days, without looking at the solution.

Rules

  • One problem per day is enough if the review is honest.
  • After 18 minutes stuck, take one hint or read only the editorial idea, then implement from memory.
  • Prefer Mediums, but use an Easy when the pattern itself is shaky.
  • Keep a retry list. Re-solving failures is where the month compounds.

Primary Source
